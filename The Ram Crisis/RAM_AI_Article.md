# Why RAM Is Getting Really Expensive and It's Kinda Weird

## Okay So What's Going On With RAM

Um, so like, I've been thinking about this and I realized that computers are getting really expensive right now. Like, my mom wanted to get a new laptop and it was so much more expensive than I thought it would be. And apparently a lot of that has to do with RAM. Which is like, random access memory or whatever. I don't really think about it that much but it's basically the thing that makes your computer remember stuff while you're using it.

Like, imagine if your brain could only remember things while you were actively thinking about them. That's kinda what RAM does but for computers. It lets your computer hold onto information really quick so you can do stuff. Without it your computer would be like, super slow and nothing would work.

Anyway, the price of RAM went up like a crazy amount. I think it was like 50% more expensive or something in 2025. That's a lot. And the reason is AI. Yeah, artificial intelligence. That's the whole thing that's making everything so expensive apparently.

## AI Needs Like, SO Much Memory

Okay so AI is this thing that can basically think? Like ChatGPT and those AI art generators and stuff. And the thing about AI is that it needs to remember a TON of information all at the same time. Like, imagine if you had to know every single word from like, a million books all at once and you had to think about all of them together. That's what AI does. It's crazy.

So AI servers, which are basically just super powerful computers that run AI stuff, they need like, way more memory than a normal computer. My computer at home probably has like 16 or 32 gigabytes of RAM which sounds like a lot to me. But AI servers have terabytes. A terabyte is like 1,000 gigabytes. So we're talking like, thousands of times more. It's insane when you think about it.

## Companies Are Just Buying All The RAM

So when all these big companies like Google and Microsoft and Amazon realized they could make money off AI, they started building these huge data centers for it. And they started buying RAM like crazy. Like, CRAZY. More RAM than they'd ever bought before. Probably more RAM than anyone had ever bought in history or something.

But here's the thing. The places that make RAM, they can't just suddenly make that much more. They have to plan ahead and stuff. So when all these companies started buying like, all of it at once, there wasn't enough. Supply and demand. I learned about that in economics class I guess. When everyone wants something and there's not enough, the price goes up.

So the price went up. A LOT.

## Factories Would Rather Make Expensive Memory

Okay so this is actually pretty cool when you think about it. The companies that make RAM can make two different kinds. They can make regular RAM for like, laptops and phones and stuff. Or they can make special memory for AI called HBM or High Bandwidth Memory or something. And HBM is like, way more expensive. They make so much more money from it.

So obviously they're gonna choose to make the expensive stuff, right? I mean, if I was a company I'd do the same thing. So they started making less regular RAM and more of the expensive AI memory. Which means there's less RAM for like, regular people. And when there's less of something everyone wants, the price goes up even more.

It's like if your school cafeteria suddenly decided to only make pizza because it makes them more money, and then everyone who wanted a sandwich is out of luck and has to like, find pizza somewhere else and pay a bunch.

## So Like, There's Just Not Enough Memory

Because AI servers use SO much memory, like way more than normal computers, the RAM just runs out really fast. An AI server could use like, as much as 1,000 regular computers. So even if factories are trying to make more, there's still not enough. The whole world is like, out of memory basically.

And that affects everything. Your laptop. Your phone. Your TV. Your gaming console. Pretty much anything that has a computer in it needs memory. So prices went up for like, everything. It's kinda crazy.

## Rich AI Companies Just Pay Whatever

Oh and here's another thing that makes it worse. AI companies are making SO much money. Like, ridiculous amounts of money. So when they need more memory, they just like, buy it? They don't even really care if it costs a bunch because they have so much money. They'll just pay whatever.

But that makes the problem worse for everyone else. Because if the biggest companies are willing to pay crazy prices, then the prices for everyone go up. It's not fair to like, regular people who just want to upgrade their computer.

## Apparently It's Gonna Stay Expensive For A While

Tech people are calling this whole thing a "memory supercycle" or whatever. Basically that means prices are probably gonna stay high for like, years. Because AI isn't going away. If anything it's getting bigger. So the demand for memory is just gonna keep going up and up.

## How This Affects Like, Me And You

So this might seem like just a nerd thing but it actually affects regular people. Gaming PCs cost way more now. If you want a new laptop it's gonna cost you like, a lot more than it did a few years ago. Even phones are getting more expensive because of this. People are just keeping their old computers way longer because new ones are too expensive.

## What's Gonna Happen

Um, so like, will this ever get better? I guess maybe? Companies are trying to make more memory. There's like, new technology that might help someday. Some countries are even building their own factories so they don't have to buy from other countries.

But AI is not stopping. It's only getting bigger. So yeah, the shortage is probably gonna stick around for a while.

## So Basically

Okay so to like, sum this all up. AI needs tons and tons of memory. Big companies are buying all of it because they have money. Factories would rather make expensive memory than regular memory. So there's not enough. And that makes everything expensive.

It's actually pretty wild when you think about it. This technology that didn't even exist like 10 years ago is now making computers way more expensive for everyone. AI is like, changing the whole computer world and we're all just dealing with it.

If you need to get a new computer or something, maybe like, wait for a sale? Because AI is here and it's making everything way more expensive and there's probably not much we can do about it.
